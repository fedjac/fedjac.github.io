FeedRepublic

As a project at Hyper Island, me and four class mates created an instagram analysis platform. It all started from the idea that we wanted to detect if someone of our friends were falsely using the #nofilter tag. The concept is simple, you just log in with your instagram account and you will quickly get an overview of your whole feed.

Role: Concept Development
